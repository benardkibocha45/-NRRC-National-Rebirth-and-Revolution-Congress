Who We Are

Welcome to National Rebirth and Revolution Congress

"Katiba, Ukweli na Haki" - Our commitment to Constitution, Truth and Justice

The National Rebirth and Revolution Congress (NRRC) is a new progressive political party in Kenya founded on the principles of good governance, social justice, and economic empowerment for all citizens. We believe in a Kenya where the constitution is respected, truth prevails in all matters of public interest, and justice is accessible to all regardless of social status.

Our movement brings together Kenyans from all walks of life who are committed to building a nation that works for everyone. We stand for integrity in leadership, equitable distribution of resources, and the protection of fundamental rights and freedoms as enshrined in our constitution.

Our Agenda for Kenya

The National Rebirth and Revolution Congress is committed to addressing these key issues through
practical, people-centered solutions and policies.

"The time for transformative leadership is now"

We believe Kenya stands at a crossroads. The old ways of doing politics have failed the majority of our people. NRRC offers a new path forward - one built on the principles of Katiba (Constitution), Ukweli (Truth) and Haki (Justice).

Our agenda is not about power for its own sake, but about using political leadership as a tool for genuine national transformation that benefits all Kenyans equally.

CONSTITUTIONAL REFORM & RULE OF LAW

We will champion full implementation of the 2010 Constitution and necessary amendments to strengthen devolution, protect citizens' rights, and ensure all leaders are accountable to the people.

ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT

Creating opportunities for all Kenyans through job creation, support for SMEs, equitable access to resources, and policies that promote shared prosperity rather than elite capture.

SOCIAL JUSTICE & EQUITY

Ensuring all Kenyans have equal access to quality education, healthcare, housing and social protection regardless of ethnicity, gender or social status.

ANTI-CORRUPTION & GOOD GOVERNANCE

Implementing strong anti-corruption measures, transparent procurement processes, and accountability systems to ensure public resources benefit all citizens.
